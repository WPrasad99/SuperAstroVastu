Frequently Asked Questions

Astrology is the study of celestial bodies and their influence on human life. It interprets the positions of planets and stars to understand life's patterns.

Astrology provides trends and insights rather than absolute predictions. It serves as a guide for understanding cosmic influences.

General horoscopes offer basic insights, while personalized readings based on your birth chart are more precise and accurate.

You can book a consultation through the "Book Appointment" section on our website. We offer online and in-person sessions.

A birth chart, or natal chart, is a map of the planets and celestial positions at the exact time and location of your birth. It provides deep insights into your personality and destiny.

Astrology is considered a metaphysical science. It combines observation and interpretation of celestial movements to provide guidance.
